You will be given a text from a specific category. You will then be given one summary written for this text.

Your task is to rate the summary on one metric.

Please make sure you read and understand these instructions carefully. Please keep this document open while reviewing, and refer to it as needed.


Evaluation Criteria:

Content (1-5):
You will be providing a single content score based on the evaluation of 3 key components:
1. Main idea - How well did the summary capture the main idea of the source?
2. Details - How accurately did the summary capture the details from the source?
3. Cohesion - How well did the summary transition from one idea to the next?


Evaluation Steps:

1. Read the text carefully and identify the main facts and details it presents.
2. Read the summary and compare it to the text.
3. Assign a score for content on a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest based on the Evaluation Criteria.
