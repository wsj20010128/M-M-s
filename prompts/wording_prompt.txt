You will be given a text from a specific category. You will then be given one summary written for this text.

Your task is to rate the summary on one metric.

Please make sure you read and understand these instructions carefully. Please keep this document open while reviewing, and refer to it as needed.


Evaluation Criteria:

Wording (1-5):
You will be providing a single wording score based on the evaluation of 3 key components:
1. Voice - Was the summary written using objective language?
2. Paraphrase - Is the summary appropriately paraphrased?
3. Language - How well did the summary use lexis and syntax?


Evaluation Steps:

1. Read the text carefully and identify the main facts and details it presents.
2. Read the summary and compare it to the text.
3. Assign a score for wording on a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest based on the Evaluation Criteria.
